A home for my Projects

Here you'll find a list of the projects I'm currently working on, as well as those I have completed

Epidemic Simulation

Simulation of disease being spread within a civilisation containing different sub-classes of 'Cells' which represent different types of people within society. Created with Java, including a GUI, achieving an A grade for KCL Informatics. FIND HERE

World Of Zuul

Fully Java-written text based game centering around a unorganized student at campus, created in Java. Objective is to collect the pages of an assignment lost and scattered around various rooms. Achieved an A grade at KCL Informatics.

Covid GUI

An easy to read visualisation of real-world COVID data within London, with calculated statistics reporting on the deaths, new cases, grocery as well as transport traffic. Converts an intial CSV file into an understandable, visual representation of London as a whole during the pandemic.
